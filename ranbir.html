Ranbir Kapoor is an Indian actor who is known for his work in Hindi films. Here are some things about him: Birth: Born on September 28, 1982 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India Family: Son of Rishi Kapoor and Neetu Singh, and nephew of actors Randhir Kapoor, Rajiv Kapoor, and Kunal Kapoor Education: Studied film production and method acting at the School of Visual Arts and Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute Career: Made his acting debut in Sanjay Leela Bhansali's Saaweriya (2007).
